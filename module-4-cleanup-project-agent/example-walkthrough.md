# Example Walkthrough — Brightwave Café Cleanup

**Module 4 — Balance Operations Co. AI Bookkeeper Toolkit™**

This picks up the same fictional client from Module 1 ([Brightwave Café](../module-1-client-onboarding-agent/example-walkthrough.md))
and runs the **Cleanup Project Agent** to turn the diagnosis into a sellable
engagement. Numbers are illustrative.

---

## The inputs we gave the Agent

**CLEANUP PLAN** — pasted directly from Module 1's output (abbreviated): CC unreconciled
~9 mo; Toast deposits booked net; ~$31k uncategorized; payroll as lump-sum transfers;
personal expenses mixed in; OBE ~$11,200; ~160-account COA with duplicates.

**RISK ASSESSMENT** — pasted from Module 1: overall **Moderate–High**; recommend
fixed-fee, phased, +15% contingency, quote 2024 first (deadline-driven).

**VOLUME METRICS:**
```text
Period: Jan 2024 – Jun 2025 (18 months), crosses 2024/2025 tax years. Cash basis.
Tax deadline: 2024 return not yet filed.
Accounts: 1 checking (unreconciled since Mar 2024 ≈ 15 mo), 1 credit card (never
reconciled ≈ 18 mo). All statements available.
~450 txns/month. ~$31k in Uncategorized + Ask My Accountant. Correcting entries: High.
Payroll: Gusto, 6 employees, lump-sum. Sales tax: TX, filed quarterly, not tied to QBO.
OBE ~$11,200. COA ~160 accounts. Some commingling. Responsiveness: average.
Software: QBO Simple Start.
```

**PRICING MODEL:**
```text
Tiered fixed-fee packages from the hours estimate. Target effective rate $75/hr.
Bill 50% deposit / 50% on completion. +25% uplift on the high end. Change order if
volume exceeds stated metrics by >15%. Done-for-You monthly at $450/mo.
```

---

## The Agent's clarifying questions (Step 1)

> A few things that change the price before I scope:
> 1. Confirm we're cleaning the full 18 months now, or 2024 first (deadline) then 2025 YTD as Phase 2 of billing?
> 2. Are the Toast payout reports (gross sales / fees / tips / sales tax) available for the full period?
> 3. Is inventory (retail beans) tracked, or expensed? (affects COGS work)
> 4. Do you need class/location tracking? (Simple Start doesn't support it — may require a plan upgrade)
> 5. Confirm your $75/hr target is the effective floor, not a list rate.

**Our answers:** Clean all 18 months but quote 2024 as the priority and bill in two
stages. Toast reports available. Beans expensed, no inventory. No class tracking needed.
$75/hr is the floor.

---

## Deliverable 1 — Cleanup Scope (abbreviated)

**In scope:** reconcile checking (15 mo) + credit card (18 mo) through Jun 2025; clear
~$31k uncategorized; build monthly Toast deposit-split entries (gross sales / fees /
tips payable / sales-tax payable); map Gusto payroll; reclass personal spend to owner's
draw; tie TX sales-tax liability to filed returns; clean COA; clear OBE; comparative
P&L + Balance Sheet; CPA-ready 2024 handoff.

**Out of scope:** 2024 tax return prep/filing (→ CPA); prior-year amendments; inventory
valuation; class/location setup; ongoing monthly bookkeeping (see Option C); anything
before Jan 2024.

**Definition of done:** both accounts reconciled through Jun 2025, $0 unexplained;
$0 uncategorized; OBE cleared; sales tax ties to filings; 2024 CPA-ready.

ASSUMPTION: all statements + Toast reports available; client answers within 3 business days.
