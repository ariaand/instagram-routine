## How to use these

Each toolkit is a self-contained markdown file with copy-paste AI prompts (use
`{{PLACEHOLDERS}}`) and ready-to-use templates. Open the one you need and follow its
quick-start.

## Selling them separately

These work well as:
- **Lead magnets** — give away a slice (e.g., the cleanup audit checklist) to grow your list.
- **Order bumps / upsells** — offer at checkout alongside the main toolkit.
- **Standalone mini-products** — package any one as its own $27–$97 offer.

## ⚠️ Same ground rules as the rest of the toolkit
- **Anonymize client data**; summarize rather than dumping full ledgers.
- **Use a business/team AI account** with data-retention controls for real client work.
- **You review everything** before it's posted, sent, or relied on.
- Nothing here is tax or legal advice — flag those to a CPA/attorney.
