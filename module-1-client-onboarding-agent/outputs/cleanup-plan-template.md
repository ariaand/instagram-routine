### Phased plan

**Phase 1 — Foundation (cash is king)**
- Connect/repair all bank & credit-card feeds.
- Reconcile every bank and credit-card account through {{MONTH}}.
- Confirm beginning balances tie to the last tax return / prior reconciliation.

**Phase 2 — Categorize & correct**
- Clear Uncategorized Expense/Income and "Ask My Accountant."
- Rebuild/clean the chart of accounts; merge duplicates.
- Map payroll to wage/tax accounts; fix owner draws vs. expenses.
- Resolve Opening Balance Equity.

**Phase 3 — Refine & verify**
- Review AR/AP aging for accuracy; write off truly uncollectible items (with client approval).
- Confirm sales tax liability ties to filings.
- Run comparative P&L and Balance Sheet; investigate anomalies.
- Lock/close each cleaned period.

### What "done" looks like
- Every bank and credit-card account reconciled through {{MONTH}} with $0 unexplained difference.
- $0 in Uncategorized / Ask My Accountant; Opening Balance Equity cleared.
- Balance Sheet ties to source documents; P&L is comparable month-over-month.
- A clean cutoff date from which monthly bookkeeping proceeds.
