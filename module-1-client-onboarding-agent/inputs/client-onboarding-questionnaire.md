# Client Onboarding Questionnaire

**Module 1 input — Balance Operations Co. AI Bookkeeper Toolkit™**

**How to use:** Send this to a new client before your kickoff call (paste it into an
email, a Google Form, or your client portal). When they respond, paste their answers
into the **Client Onboarding Agent** as the `CLIENT QUESTIONNAIRE` input.

> Tip: You don't need every field answered to run the Agent — it will ask follow-up
> questions about anything important that's missing.
