# Starter SOP Library — What to Document First

**Module 5 — Balance Operations Co. AI Bookkeeper Toolkit™**

**How to use:** These are the 10 SOPs every bookkeeping firm should have. Don't try to
write them all at once — run one through the **SOP Builder Agent** per week and you'll
have a fully documented firm in about three months. Several can be built straight from
checklists the other toolkit modules already produce.

---

| # | SOP | Why it's high-value | Shortcut |

## How to build each one

For each SOP:
1. Open the **SOP Builder Agent**.
2. Brain-dump how you do it today (or paste the relevant checklist from Modules 1/2/4).
3. Answer the Agent's interview questions.
4. Review, confirm the "ASSUMPTION — confirm" flags, and save the SOP + checklist +
   training doc to your firm wiki.

```
Firm Process Library/
├── 01-onboarding/        (SOP · checklist · training)
├── 02-weekly-categorization/
├── 03-reconciliation/
├── 04-month-end-close/
├── 05-cleanup-projects/
├── 06-accounts-payable/
├── 07-accounts-receivable/
├── 08-client-reporting/
├── 09-payroll/
└── 10-year-end-1099/
```

Each folder holds the three documents the Agent produces. Once it's built, you can hire,
delegate, and take a vacation without the firm skipping a beat.

---

## Keeping SOPs alive
- **Review quarterly.** Processes drift; re-run changed workflows through the Agent and
  bump the revision log.
- **One source of truth.** Link the checklist in your PM tool back to the full SOP in the
  wiki so they never diverge.
- **Onboard with the training docs.** New hires read the training doc, then work from the
  checklist, then graduate to the SOP as reference.
