# Example Walkthrough — Brightwave Café Monthly Engagement

**Module 6 — Balance Operations Co. AI Bookkeeper Toolkit™**

This continues the same fictional client from Modules 1 and 4
([Brightwave Café](../module-1-client-onboarding-agent/example-walkthrough.md)).
The cleanup (Module 4) is sold; now we price the **recurring monthly** engagement that
follows it. Numbers are illustrative.

---

## The inputs we gave the Agent

**INDUSTRY:**
```text
Single-location café + small retail (coffee beans). Toast POS. TX sales tax.
```

**REVENUE:**
```text
~$640k/yr, growing.
```

**COMPLEXITY** — pasted from Module 1's Risk Assessment + intake (abbreviated):
```text
~450 txns/mo. 1 checking + 1 credit card reconciled monthly. Payroll via Gusto
(6 employees) — record only, not run. TX sales tax — wants tracking, would like us to
file. No inventory tracking. Single entity, no class tracking. Cash basis. Toast
payouts need monthly split entries (sales/fees/tips/tax). Cleanup handled separately.
Reporting: monthly statements + a quarterly call. Close by the 15th. Medium touch.
```

**SERVICE TIERS** — pasted the firm's Starter/Growth/Premium structure (Starter $450 /
Growth $750 / Premium $1,500; sales-tax filing add-on $100/mo; setup $500; $75/hr floor).

---

## The Agent's clarifying questions (Step 1)

> A few things that affect the tier and price:
> 1. Do you want us to run payroll, or just record what Gusto produces? (You said record-only — confirming.)
> 2. Sales tax: track only, or file the TX returns too?
> 3. Is the Toast deposit-split work ongoing every month? (It adds recurring categorization time.)
> 4. Any management reporting/KPIs beyond standard statements, or are statements + a quarterly call enough?
> 5. Confirm $75/hr is your effective floor and Growth is $750.

**Our answers:** Record payroll only. File TX sales tax (add-on). Yes, Toast splits are
monthly/ongoing. Statements + quarterly call is enough for now. Floor confirmed.

---

## Deliverable 1 — Service Package (abbreviated)

**Recommendation: Growth ($750/mo).** ~450 txns/mo, 2 accounts, payroll recording, and
ongoing Toast split entries put this clearly above Starter; advisory/dashboard (Premium)
isn't needed yet.

| Service | Starter | **Growth** ⭐ | Premium |
|---------|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Reconciliation | 2 accts | up to 4 | 5+ |
| Categorization | ~150/mo | ~400/mo | high vol |
| Payroll recording | — | ✅ | ✅ |
| Sales-tax tracking | — | 1 state | multi |
| Reporting | statements | + summary | dashboard |
| Advisory | — | quarterly call | monthly |
| Close by | 20th | 15th | 10th |

**Recommended add-on:** + TX sales-tax filing ($100/mo) — they collect but don't want
to touch returns.
**Setup:** $500 one-time. **Cleanup prerequisite:** Yes — sold separately (Module 4).

## Deliverable 2 — Customized Proposal (abbreviated, client-facing)

> **What you get each month:** clean, current books; P&L and Balance Sheet in your inbox
> by the 15th; your Toast sales, tips, and fees sorted correctly; payroll recorded; TX
> sales tax tracked and filed; and a quarterly call to talk through the numbers.

| | Starter | **Growth** ⭐ | Premium |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Monthly** | **$450** | **$750** | **$1,500** |
| Best for | Very simple | Growing cafés like yours | $1M+, advisory |

> ⭐ I recommend **Growth** — it covers everything you described without paying for
> advisory you don't need yet. **+ $100/mo** and we'll file your TX sales tax too.

Getting started: $500 setup; your books need a quick cleanup first (separate proposal
attached); first clean close by the 15th of your first full month. Billed monthly in
advance, 12-month term, 30 days' notice to cancel.

---

## Deliverable 3 — Pricing Recommendation (abbreviated, internal)

**Rationale:** ~450 txns/mo + monthly Toast splits + payroll recording ≈ **7 hrs/mo**
to deliver. Revenue ~$640k supports value pricing well above the hours floor. Growth fits.

| Item | Estimate |
|------|----------|
| Monthly delivery hours | ~7 hrs |
| Floor rate | $75/hr |
| **Cost floor** | ~$525/mo |
| **Recommended price** | **$750/mo** (+$100 sales-tax filing) |
| Implied effective rate | ~$107/hr (~$121 with add-on) |

Floor: don't go below **$650/mo** for this scope. Re-price if volume grows >25%, a
second location/class is added, or at the annual review. Cleanup billed separately (Module 4).

> Sanity check: $750 ÷ ~7 hrs ≈ $107/hr — clears the $75 floor comfortably. ✅
