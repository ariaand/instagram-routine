# Proposal Intake Checklist

**Module 6 input — Balance Operations Co. AI Bookkeeper Toolkit™**

**How to use:** Capture these before running the Proposal Generator. The three headline
inputs are **industry, revenue, and complexity** — but complexity is where the price
really lives. Paste the summary into the Agent. If you ran Module 1, the complexity
section is already in your Risk Assessment.

> 🔒 You only need bands, counts, and flags — never raw client data.

---

## The three headline inputs

### 1. Industry
- [ ] Industry / what the business does: ______
- [ ] Industry-specific work (POS payouts, e-comm platforms, job costing, etc.): ______

### 2. Revenue
- [ ] Annual revenue (or band): ☐ <$250k ☐ $250k–$1M ☐ $1M–$5M ☐ $5M+
- [ ] Growing / stable / declining: ______

### 3. Complexity (the real price driver)
- [ ] Monthly transaction volume (rough # of bank/card transactions): ______
- [ ] # of bank accounts ______ + # of credit cards ______ to reconcile monthly
- [ ] Payroll? ☐ No ☐ Yes (provider: ______; # employees: ______)
- [ ] Sales tax? ☐ No ☐ Yes (state[s]: ______; want us to file? ☐ Yes ☐ No)
- [ ] AR/AP management wanted? ☐ Reconcile only ☐ Manage invoicing ☐ Manage bill pay
- [ ] Inventory tracked? ☐ No ☐ Yes
- [ ] Multiple entities / classes / locations / properties? ☐ No ☐ Yes (#: ______)
- [ ] Basis: ☐ Cash ☐ Accrual ☐ Needs CPA input

---

## Service expectations (shape the tier)
- [ ] Reporting depth: ☐ Basic statements ☐ Management reporting/KPIs ☐ Advisory/CFO-lite
- [ ] Close timeline expected: ☐ By the 10th ☐ By the 15th ☐ By month-end ☐ Flexible
- [ ] Meeting cadence: ☐ None ☐ Quarterly ☐ Monthly call
- [ ] Support/response expectation: ☐ Standard ☐ Priority
- [ ] Software & plan: ______ (does the plan support what's needed?)

## Engagement factors
- [ ] Does the client need a **cleanup/catch-up first**? ☐ No ☐ Yes → sell separately (Module 4)
- [ ] Client touch level (questions/meetings): ☐ Low ☐ Medium ☐ High
- [ ] Ability/willingness to pay (gut read): ☐ Price-sensitive ☐ Value-focused
- [ ] Target start date: ______

---

## How to summarize for the Agent

```text
INDUSTRY: Single-location café + small retail (coffee beans). Toast POS.

REVENUE: ~$640k/yr, growing.

COMPLEXITY: ~450 txns/mo. 1 checking + 1 credit card to reconcile monthly. Payroll via
Gusto (6 employees) — record only, not run. TX sales tax — wants us to track, not file.
No inventory tracking. Single entity, no class tracking. Cash basis. Needs cleanup first
(handled as a separate engagement). Reporting: monthly statements + a quarterly call.
Close by the 15th. Medium touch. Value-focused.

SERVICE TIERS: [paste your Starter/Growth/Premium definitions + prices]
```

That gives the Agent everything it needs to recommend a tier and price it defensibly.

---

## Quick reference — what each driver does to the monthly fee
| Driver ↑ | Effect on monthly fee |
|----------|----------------------|
| Higher transaction volume | #1 driver — more categorization/recon time |
| More bank/card accounts | More monthly reconciliations |
| Higher revenue | Higher stakes + ability to pay → supports value pricing |
| Payroll / sales tax / inventory / multi-entity | Each adds a scope block or add-on |
| Deeper reporting / advisory / meetings | Moves the tier up |
| High touch / priority support | Premium tier or add-on |
