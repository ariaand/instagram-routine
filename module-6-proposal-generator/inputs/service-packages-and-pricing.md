# Service Packages & Pricing

**Module 6 input — Balance Operations Co. AI Bookkeeper Toolkit™**

**How to use:** Define your recurring monthly tiers and pricing here, then paste the
relevant block into the Proposal Generator as the `SERVICE TIERS` input. A starter
template is below — **edit the prices to your market.** If you leave tiers blank, the
Agent proposes a structure with prices clearly labeled as placeholders for you to confirm.

> These are frameworks, not a price list. Your niche, market, and experience set the
> numbers. The Agent recommends; **you set the final price.**

## Starter tier structure (edit to your firm)

```text
SERVICE TIERS:

STARTER — {{$300–$500/mo}}  | Best for: solopreneurs / very small, simple businesses
- Monthly reconciliation of up to 2 accounts (1 bank + 1 card)
- Transaction categorization (up to ~150 txns/mo)
- Monthly P&L and Balance Sheet
- Books closed by the 20th
- Email support (2 business-day response)

GROWTH — {{$600–$1,000/mo}}  | Best for: established small businesses, some complexity
- Everything in Starter, plus:
- Reconciliation of up to 4 accounts
- Categorization (up to ~400 txns/mo)
- AR/AP tracking; payroll recording (provider integration)
- Sales-tax tracking (1 state)
- Monthly statements + a short management summary
- Books closed by the 15th; quarterly check-in call
- Priority email support (1 business-day response)

PREMIUM — {{$1,200–$2,500+/mo}}  | Best for: $1M+ revenue, multi-account/complex, advisory
- Everything in Growth, plus:
- Reconciliation of 5+ accounts; high transaction volume
- Full AR/AP management; multi-state sales-tax tracking
- Class/location or multi-entity tracking
- Management reporting / KPI dashboard; monthly advisory call
- Books closed by the 10th
- Priority support (same-day response)

ADD-ONS (a la carte):
- Sales-tax filing: {{+$75–$150/mo per state}}
- Payroll processing (run, not just record): {{+$X/mo}}
- 1099 filing (annual): {{+$X}}
- Class/location tracking setup: {{+$X one-time}}
- Catch-up of current year: {{quote via Module 4}}

SETUP / ONBOARDING FEE: {{$300–$750 one-time}}
CLEANUP (if books aren't current): sold separately as a project — see Module 4.

PRICING GUARDRAILS:
- Target effective rate floor: {{$75/hr}}. Never quote a monthly fee implying less.
- Price on value/scope, not hours, in client-facing text.
- Bill monthly in advance via auto-pay. 12-month term with annual price review.
- Pricing assumes the stated transaction volume; re-price if volume grows >25%.
- Do not bundle cleanup into the monthly fee.
```

## Pricing approaches (pick how you set the tier prices)

### Approach 1 — Value/scope tiers (recommended)
Set three fixed monthly prices by tier (as above). Fast to quote, easy to deliver,
scales with productized SOPs (Module 5). The Agent maps the client to the right tier.

### Approach 2 — Hours floor → value price
Estimate monthly delivery hours × target effective rate = floor; price the value above
it. Use this to sanity-check tier prices, not as the client-facing logic.

### Approach 3 — Revenue-banded
Anchor minimums to client revenue (e.g., businesses $1M–$5M start at Growth). Useful as
a guardrail so you don't under-tier a large, complex client.

| Client profile | Rough monthly range |
|----------------|--------------------|
| Solo / very simple, <$250k rev, ~100 txns | Starter |
| Established small biz, payroll + 1 sales-tax state, ~300 txns | Growth |
