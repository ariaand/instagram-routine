# Month-End Close Intake Checklist

**Module 2 input — Balance Operations Co. AI Bookkeeper Toolkit™**

**How to use:** Gather these data points at close time, then paste the summary into the
Month-End Close Agent. The two headline inputs are **bank/CC balances** and
**reconciliation notes** — everything else sharpens the journal-entry suggestions.

> 🔒 You only need ending balances, statuses, and notes — never the full register.

## How to summarize for the Agent

```text
CLIENT/PERIOD: Brightwave Café (single-location café + retail). Cash basis. Closing June 2026.

BANK & CC BALANCES:
- Checking: statement $18,200 / book $18,200 — reconciled. ✅
- Credit card: statement $4,650 / book $4,910 — NOT reconciled, diff $260.

RECONCILIATION NOTES:
- Checking clean. CC difference appears to be a duplicate $260 supplies charge — confirm.
- Toast June payouts booked but not yet split into sales/fees/tips/sales tax.
- One $1,200 charge in "Ask My Accountant" — awaiting client answer.
- Undeposited funds $0.

RECURRING ITEMS:
- Depreciation $300/mo. Prepaid insurance amortization $150/mo. Payroll recorded via
  Gusto. TX sales tax tracked monthly. No rent accrual (paid on time).
```

That gives the Agent what it needs to tailor the checklist, suggest the right entries,
and draft the summary.
