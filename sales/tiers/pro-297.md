## Start here (≈30 minutes)

1. Open **`setup/README.md`** and install your most-used agents as Claude Projects or
   ChatGPT custom GPTs (paste-ready files in `setup/agent-instructions/`).
2. Set up the **Month-End Close Agent** first — you'll use it every month, for every client.
3. Set up the **Client Communication Agent** and teach it your voice (paste 2–3 of your
   real emails). It's your biggest daily time-saver.
4. Use the **SOP Builder Agent** + **`sop-library/`** to document your firm and prepare to delegate.
