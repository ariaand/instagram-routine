## FAQ

**Do I need to be technical?**
No. If you can copy and paste, you can run this. Setup takes about 10 minutes per agent
and we walk you through it.

**Does it work with ChatGPT, or only Claude?**
Both. Every agent includes paste-ready instructions for Claude Projects *and* ChatGPT
custom GPTs.

**Will the AI mess up my books?**
The agents **draft and suggest** — they don't post anything. The Month-End Close Agent,
for example, proposes journal entries for *you* to review and post. You're always in control.

**Is my client data safe?**
The Toolkit is built privacy-first: anonymize identifiers, summarize instead of dumping
ledgers, and use a business/team AI account with data-retention controls. We remind you
at every step.

**Is this tax advice?**
No. It's a bookkeeping productivity system. Anything tax/legal is explicitly flagged to
send to a CPA/attorney.
